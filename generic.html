About me

I'm Wei Kit, a Year 2 student at Republic Polytechnic that is captivated by how games work and
bring things to life. How the game characters can move, interact with the environment and other players
This passion grew to become my interest for game design and 3D asset creation

Just like many others, my 3D journey began with a donut

I specialize in skills such as 3D modelling and programming. I am constantly finding new ways to push the boundaries of interactive design. Outside of academics, I enjoy activities like cycling, playing indie games which help inspire and shape my creative process.
