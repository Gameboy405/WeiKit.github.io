About me

I'm Wei Kit, a Year 3 student at Republic Polytechnic captivated by how games work and bring things to life. Game characters moving, interacting with environments and players — this fascination became a passion for game design and 3D asset creation.

Just like many others, my 3D journey began with a donut

I specialize in skills such as 3D modelling and programming. I’m constantly finding new ways to push the boundaries of interactive design. Outside of academics, I enjoy cycling and indie games which shape my creative process.
